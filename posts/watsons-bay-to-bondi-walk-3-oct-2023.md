Sydney is blessed with some of the most amazing walks, many well within it's city limits and accessible by public transit. One such walk is the Manly to Bondi Walk. This 80 km walk follows the massive harbour shoreline from Manly Beach to Bondi Beach. The walk meanders along breath taking coastal cliffs, through tropical forests, past beautiful beaches and through quaint neighbourhoods along the way. The walk can be be easily broken into smaller manageable sections: <https://www.sydneycoastwalks.com.au/manly-to-bondi-walk/>

![](images/watsons-bay-to-bondi-walk-3-oct-2023/image_0.jpg)

Today we tackled the Watsons Bay to Bondi section. Although a couple of coastal cliff sections were closed for maintenance, the walk still allowed some beautiful vistas.
