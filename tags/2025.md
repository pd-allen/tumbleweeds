Post Date 2025-06-29 After two leisurely weeks exploring beautiful Malta, we caught our flight to Sicily, Italy. Our itinerary for the next five weeks was rather full, but we tried to maintained a somewhat relaxed pace. We began in Catania, followed by a few days in the terraced town of Taormina, in the shadow of Mount Etna. From there we pressed north-west to Palermo, before we hopped a train, on the last train-ferry, to the mainland. After a week in Naples, we proceeded to the Puglia region, the breadbasket of Italy, before finally making our way to "Rome and Home".

::: {card}Southeast Asia April 2025
:link:  /posts/southeast-asia-april-2025

Post Date 2025-05-11 Our next destination was Southeast Asia, a region unfamiliar to us. With great expectations we boarded our Scoot Airline flight from Sydney to Singapore. Our three-week adventure unfolded as follows: we started with a one day stop in Singapore, next we set sail on the Oceania Regatta for a 16-day Gilded Eastern Kingdoms cruise along the eastern coast of Asia. Major stops included Bangkok, Saigon, Hong Kong, and Taipei, with several smaller stops along the way. We wrapped up our cruise in Taipei, where we spent four days exploring the city. Unsurprisingly, it appeared that everything is "Made in Taiwan".
